Frequently Asked Questions

Are multi round tournaments supported?
Yes, you can add tournament round for each day in your tournament up to a limit of 10.
Are leagues supported?
Yes, leagues are supported with a full season leaderboard and multiple scoring formats including customizable fedex points.
Can players register themselves?
Yes, you can either add players yourself or add self registration. You can also change the entry fees and game purses, keep track of who's paid, and much more.
How many players/teams does the app support?
The app can support up to 1000 players and 500 teams.
Free sounds too good to be true, what's the catch?
No catch. This is a passion project of mine and I love building things that other people enjoy using. My only ask is to help spread the word if you enjoy it and consider leaving a rating/review on the app stores! If you'd like to help support the app in other ways you can reach out to me using the contact button.
The slope/rating/yardages/holes are incorrect for my course
You can easily edit any information in a course if it's incorrect by going to the courses tab from the home screen, tapping the 3 dots and "Edit course".
Does every player in my tournament need to sign up?
No, only one player per foursome needs to sign up to enter scores.
Does the app support handicaps?
Yes, handicaps are supported and highly customizable. You can set each user's tournament handicap before the tournament starts. You can enable course handicaps to automatically account for the slope and rating of the tee each player is playing off of. You can choose the percentage of each player's handicap to use for each round in the tournament and you can even modify each user's handicap for every round in the tournament.
Are tournament flights supported?
Yes, you can add up to 50 flights.
Can I set up scorecards/matches before the tournament starts?
Yes, you can set up each scorecard before each round by tapping the green play button beside the user/team's name.
I've added all the players to my tournament leaderboard, now what?
Just send a message to everyone with your tournament's invite code and ask them to download the app. After they download the app and sign up, they can enter the invite code to see your tournament.
Can I hide the leaderboard results from the players?
Yes, in the tournament settings you can hide the leaderboard results. This is useful if towards the end of your tournament, you want to keep the winner a secret until you announce it.
Can I add photos to my event?
Yes, you can change your main tournament photo and add as many photos as you want.
Are tournament skins supported?
Yes tournament skins are supported with a variety of options including using net scores, requiring par or better, disabling carry overs and more.
Are multiple tees supported?
Yes you can choose which tees are available to each user when they start their round.
Can I export the tournament results?
Yes you can export the tournament results and all the scorecards to a CSV file.
Can users edit any scorecard?
No users can only edit their own scorecard, including editing scores for anyone else on their scorecard. This is unless you make another user a tournament admin by tapping on their name in the leaderboard. Tournament admins can edit any scorecard.
Is there a group chat?
Yes there is a group chat where everyone in your tournament can participate.
Are hole competitions supported?
Yes you can add hole competitions like closest to the pin or longest drive. Players will see the hole competitions on their scorecard.
Are mixed formats supported?
Yes, if you choose a mixed format, you can choose a different game format for each tournament round (e.g. Scramble in round 1, Best Ball in round 2).
I have a weekly/monthly/yearly outing is there an easy way to duplicate a past event?
Yes, you can copy any tournament settings and import users from past tournaments.
Are 9 hole events supported?
Yes you can choose 9 or 18 holes for each round in your tournament.
Does the app keep track of my handicap?
Yes the app will automatically keep track of your handicap using the rounds you enter so that it's always up to date. You can also disable this feature if you want so your handicap stays the same unless you change it.
Can I add spectators to my tournament?
Yes, anyone that joins your tournament with your invite code and hasn't been added to the leaderboard automatically becomes a spectator. This means they can see the leaderboard and view scorecards, but they can't modify a scorecard.
What type of notifications are supported?
You can enable notifications for birdies or better, first place changes, match results, hole outs and more.
Is the app stable?
Thousands of successful tournaments have been run on Squabbit with hundreds of 5 star reviews and an average of 4.9 stars on the app stores. Squabbit is backed by Google's Firebase backend so there are no concerns with performance or scalability.
I don't see the feature I want, can you add it?
Yes! I'm constantly adding new features which are driven by user requests. Please reach out if you'd like to see a feature added.
