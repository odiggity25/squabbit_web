Reliable when it matters

Google Cloud infrastructure

Squabbit runs on Firebase and Google Cloud — the same infrastructure behind some of the world's largest apps. Your event won't slow down whether there are 4 players or 400.

Works without signal

Scores are saved locally on your device and sync automatically when you're back online. Patchy cell service on the course won't cost you a single stroke.

Backups and version history

Every scorecard is backed up and can be restored to any previous version. If someone accidentally overwrites a score, you can roll it back in seconds.

Frequently asked questions

Free sounds too good to be true. What's the catch?
No catch. This is a passion project and I love building things people enjoy using. There are no paywalls and no limits. If you'd like to help support the app, you can chip in here.
Are multi-round tournaments supported?
Yes, you can add a round for each day of your tournament, up to 50 rounds. Each round can use a different format if you choose a mixed format event.
How many players can a tournament or league have?
Up to 1,000 players and 500 teams per event. Whether it's a small weekend group or a large club championship, Squabbit can handle it.
Does every player need to sign up?
No, only one player per group needs to sign up to enter scores. Others can join with an invite code to follow along on the leaderboard.
Does the app support handicaps?
Yes, handicaps are fully supported and highly customizable. The app can connect to the World Handicap System, automatically track your handicap from rounds you play, calculate course handicaps from slope and rating, and let you set custom percentages per round.
Can I set up tee times and scorecards before the event?
Yes, you can use the auto scheduler to generate tee times and matchups instantly, or set them up manually. Scorecards can be pre-configured before each round starts.
Can I export the results?
Yes, you can export tournament results and all scorecards to a CSV file for your own records or reporting.
The course I'm playing at has incorrect data
You can edit any course directly in the app. Go to the Courses tab, tap the three dots on the course, and select "Edit course." Your edits become available to everyone using that course.
I don't see the feature I need. Can you add it?
New features are constantly being added based on user requests. Submit your idea on the Ideas page and vote on what others have suggested.
