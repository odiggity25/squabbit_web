Health Data (HealthKit)

The Squabbit Apple Watch companion app uses Apple HealthKit to record golf workout sessions while you play a round. This allows your round to appear as a workout in the Apple Health app.

  • Squabbit only writes golf workout sessions to HealthKit. It does not read any health data such as heart rate, step count, or medical information.
  • HealthKit access requires your explicit permission and can be revoked at any time in your device's Settings.
  • Health data is never sold, shared with third parties, or used for advertising.
  • Health data is stored locally on your device by Apple Health and is not transmitted to any external servers by Squabbit.

Location Data

Squabbit may access your device's GPS location to measure distances on the golf course (e.g., distance to the green). Location data is used only during active rounds and is not stored on external servers or shared with third parties.
