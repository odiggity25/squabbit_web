Chip In 💚

Scan a QR Code, Start Scoring

Getting every player in your tournament to download an app and create an account before their tee time can be a challenge. Now they don't have to. Players just scan the QR code on their printed scorecard or cart sign and they're immediately entering scores, viewing the leaderboard, and using the GPS course map. No download, no sign-up, no account needed.

How it works

1
Print scorecards or cart signs from your tournament. Each one has a unique QR code.
2
Players scan the QR code with their phone camera.
3
They tap their name and start scoring.

Players can enter scores for their scorecard, view the live leaderboard, use the GPS course map, and see the activity feed. When they're ready, a banner prompts them to create an account so they can keep access to their data.

To get started, go to your tournament settings and tap Print Assets.
