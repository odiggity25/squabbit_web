Embed your leaderboard on your website

Jun 9, 2026

Your standings deserve a home on your own turf. You can now embed your league or tournament leaderboard directly into your own website, so the live scores show up right where your members already go to look for them.

A new embed code in your settings

Open your group's website settings and you'll find a new embed code option. Copy the snippet, paste it into a page on your own site, and the leaderboard appears in place. No design work, no manual updates: as scores come in, the embedded leaderboard reflects them.

Made for organizers with their own site

This is built for golf societies, clubs, and event organizers who already run their own websites and want the standings to live there. Instead of sending people off to a separate link, you keep them on your page where your branding, schedule, and everything else already lives.

Built on public leaderboards

The embed builds on Squabbit's public leaderboards, so anyone visiting your site can follow along without a Squabbit account. Spectators, family, and members who haven't downloaded the app can all keep an eye on the action straight from your website.

Set it up once and the leaderboard takes care of itself. Your site becomes the place to watch the event unfold.
