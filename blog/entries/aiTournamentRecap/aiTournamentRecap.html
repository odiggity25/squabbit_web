AI tournament recaps

Jul 3, 2026

The round ends, the drinks come out, and the stories start: who won, who collapsed on the back nine, who stuck one to two feet on the closest-to-the-pin hole. Those stories deserve better than fading out by the time everyone gets home.

Squabbit can now generate an AI recap of your tournament, built from the actual results of your event. It digs through the day and pulls out the moments worth talking about: the birdie run that won it, the lead that slipped away on 16, the guy who topped the putts count again. And it has some fun along the way, poking at the collapses as happily as it celebrates the wins.

Pick your tone

Head to your tournament's stats view and generate a recap in the tone that fits your group. Banter is a sports recap with friendly ribbing, the kind of write-up your league night deserves. Professional is a clean tournament report for events that want it played straight.

It covers the whole event

The recap isn't just a list of final scores. It summarizes every format you played, calls out the hole competitions like closest to the pin and longest drive, and surfaces the notable stats from the day. If your event ran a scramble alongside individual stroke play with a couple of CTP holes sprinkled in, all of it makes the story.

Saved for everyone

Once generated, the recap is saved on the tournament, so everyone in the event opens the app and finds the write-up waiting for them. No forwarding, no copy-pasting into the group chat just so people can read it. Copy and share buttons are there too, for when a line is too good not to send around.

For weekly league nights and one-off outings alike, it turns the day's scores into a story. Run your next event on Squabbit and give the group something to argue about on the drive home.
