Public leaderboards are here!

Now people can see your league or tournament leaderboard without having to create an account on Squabbit. This simplified version of your leaderboard can be viewed from a URL meaning you can also embed it into your own website.

You can access your league or tournament's leaderboard URL by opening the settings -> Other -> Public leaderboard URL. Only people you share your leaderboard URL with will be able to see it.

Being a simplified version of the leaderboard means that some features like real time updates and flights are not supported. Users can tap the “See more in the app” button at the bottom to see the full version of the leaderboard in the app.
