How can I make another player an admin?

In your tournament, league, or club, tap the gear icon → Players, then tap the Role column for the player you want to promote.

Different teams in each round?

Add the same format multiple times and enable each one for a single round. Then create different teams in each format.

How can I see net and gross leaderboards?

Add Stroke Play twice — once with handicaps enabled and once with handicaps disabled.
