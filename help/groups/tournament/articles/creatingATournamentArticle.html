Creating a Tournament

Groups tab

In the Groups tab, you can organize players into different groups. For more detailed information, refer to our Creating a League article.

Choosing the format

When choosing the format of your tournament, you have several options to consider. Whether it's a single elimination, double elimination, round robin, or another format, make sure to choose the one that best suits your needs.

Adding players

Adding players to your tournament is simple. Navigate to the Players tab, and you can either manually add players or import a list of players from a CSV file.
