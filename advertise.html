Frequently asked questions

What can I advertise?

The home-screen slot is the only sponsored content in the app, so we keep the bar high. Anything that would feel out of place to a golfer or harm trust gets declined.

We accept

  • Golf brands, courses, and tournament services
  • Fantasy golf, DFS, prediction games (where legal)
  • Apparel, equipment, accessories
  • Travel, instruction, and indoor simulator businesses
  • Charities and nonprofit golf events

We don't accept

  • Sportsbooks or anything regulated as gambling in the user's region
  • Misleading offers, "free trial" dark patterns, or chargeback traps
  • Adult, alcohol-as-primary-product, tobacco, or weapons
  • Crypto, NFT, and most fintech promotions
  • Competing tournament-management or scoring apps
How is the ad priced?
Priced on a simple CPM (cost per 1,000 impressions), at $15 CPM. You set the budget, and your ad runs until it's spent — so a slower stretch just means it runs a little longer to deliver the impressions you paid for. There's no auction and no bidding.
Can my ad be exclusive while it runs?
There's a single ad slot on the home screen, and while your campaign runs it rotates among the advertisers live at the same time. Because you buy a set volume of impressions at a fixed CPM rather than bidding for the slot, you always get the reach you paid for, no matter who else is running. If you want sole occupancy for a specific window, get in touch and we can arrange an exclusive.
What's the click-through experience?
Tapping the ad opens your destination URL in the user's default browser. We don't deep-link into other apps and we don't require any SDK or pixel install on your end.
What reporting do I get?
Your advertiser portal shows impressions, clicks, and click-through rate live throughout the flight, plus a day-by-day graph of views and clicks. If you provide UTM parameters on your destination URL, you can also see Squabbit traffic directly in your own analytics.
Can I update the creative mid-flight?
Yes. Swap the image or video right in your portal whenever you like, and the change rolls out across every platform. No need to email us.
Do you charge to design the creative?
We don't run a creative shop, but if you're stuck we can give you a one-page brief with examples that have worked. Most advertisers ship something good on their first try.
Is this the same as advertising in your app stores?
No. This places your ad inside Squabbit, where the active users actually live. App-store ads reach people searching for new apps; this reaches people who already open Squabbit when they play.
