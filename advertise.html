What we accept

We're picky on purpose.

The home screen ad slot is the only sponsored content in the app, so we keep the bar high. Anything that would feel out-of-place to a golfer or harm trust gets declined.

We accept

  • Golf brands, courses, and tournament services
  • Fantasy golf, DFS, prediction games (where legal)
  • Apparel, equipment, accessories
  • Travel, instruction, and indoor simulator businesses
  • Charities and nonprofit golf events

We don't accept

  • Sportsbooks or anything regulated as gambling in the user's region
  • Misleading offers, "free trial" dark patterns, or chargeback traps
  • Adult, alcohol-as-primary-product, tobacco, or weapons
  • Crypto, NFT, and most fintech promotions
  • Competing tournament-management or scoring apps

Frequently asked questions

Can my ad be exclusive while it runs?
Yes. There's a single ad slot on the home screen, so each flight is naturally exclusive. If multiple advertisers are scheduled in the same window, we either rotate evenly or stagger flights — we'll be upfront about which it is when you book.
What's the click-through experience?
Tapping the ad opens your destination URL in the user's default browser. We don't deep-link into other apps and we don't require any SDK or pixel install on your end.
What reporting do I get?
At the end of each flight you'll get total impressions, unique reach, and click-through count. If you provide UTM parameters on your destination URL, you can also see Squabbit traffic directly in your own analytics.
Can I update the creative mid-flight?
Yes. Send a new image and we'll roll it out to all platforms within 24 hours.
Can you help with the creative?
We don't run a creative shop, but if you're stuck we can share examples that have worked and review your draft before it goes live. Most advertisers ship something good on their first try.
Is this the same as advertising in your app stores?
No. This places your ad inside Squabbit, where the active users actually live. App-store ads reach people searching for new apps; this reaches people who already open Squabbit every weekend.
