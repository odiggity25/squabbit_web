Name

Always write Squabbit with a capital S. Never write it as "squabbit", "SQUABBIT", or "SquAbbit".

Usage Guidelines

Do

  • Use the logo on a clean, uncluttered background
  • Maintain clear space around the logo
  • Link back to squabbitgolf.com when using the logo
  • Use the provided brand colors

Don't

  • Stretch, rotate, or distort the logo
  • Change the logo colors
  • Place the logo on busy or low-contrast backgrounds
  • Use the logo to imply endorsement without permission
