If you would like to delete your Squabbit account and all of your account information you have two options:

1. Log into your account -> open the profile tab -> tap the 3 dots -> Profile settings -> Delete account

2. You can also contact us at support@squabbitgolf.com and we can delete your account after verifying your email address.

In both scenarios all your account information including authentication details and your golf rounds are immediately and permanently deleted.
