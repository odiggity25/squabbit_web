Release Notes

What's new in Squabbit. Every release ships to the App Store and Google Play, and the web app updates automatically.

6.6.1 – 6.6.2 June 9, 2026 Latest
  • Live leaderboards on your lock screen, updating as scores come in
  • Plays-like distances: club recommendations now factor in elevation, live weather, and lie
  • Private profiles with follow requests: approve who can see your rounds, stats, and activity
  • New admin activity feed and audit log so group admins can see exactly what changed and who did it
  • Embed your league or tournament leaderboard on your own website
  • Sticky leaderboard column headers, plus expand or collapse individual teams
  • Per-hole team scores on Nassau scorecards
  • Add a photo or share your round right from the bottom of the scorecard
  • Ryder Cup match shuffle now minimizes repeat opponents
  • Import player handicaps from your CSV for everyone, including registered players
  • Fixed net 9-hole stats double-counting your handicap
  • Fixed registration windows using the wrong clock across time zones
6.6.0 June 2, 2026
  • Completely revamped fees and payments: track who owes what, split shared expenses, and settle up with a simplified-debts view that minimizes the number of payments
  • Pay and get paid with Venmo, Stripe, or your own custom payment methods
  • Settle side-game bets right from the scorecard
  • New Players page with a unified field editor, plus searchable settings to find any option fast
  • Improved participant picker: search, sort, select all/none, and copy players from another format
  • League option to use your official WHS/GHIN handicap instead of the app-computed one
  • Live home feed: new rounds now appear instantly without a refresh
  • Edit notes and rich text in a dedicated full-screen editor
  • Set pairwise tiebreakers and manual placements for tied players
  • Fixed Skins natural-birdie tiebreaker, the Stableford progression chart, several 9-hole handicap calculations, elimination bracket byes, and league leaderboards mixing score formats
6.5.4 May 20, 2026
  • New convert-to-league flow makes it easy to turn a tournament into a recurring league season
  • Add a "My Rounds" card on the Schedule tab for quick access to your upcoming rounds
  • Stableford leaderboards can now be ranked by total score (in addition to average)
  • Add "holes that count" support to Match Play, Singles Match Play, and Low Ball/High Ball
  • Add blinds to Ryder Cup, Team Match Play, and Team Elimination team formats
  • Tap to call or text any group member's phone number from the Home screen
  • Substitutes now flow through team rosters so they count in Best Ball and other team formats
  • Faster leaderboard updates during score entry
  • Reuse existing tournament players when re-importing by name from CSV
  • Fix a crash when opening a round with corrupt hole stats
  • Fix a feedback-loop crash in league bank balances
  • Fix duplicate rounds being created when adding a player to a scorecard
  • Fix handicap precision on 9-hole rounds
  • Fix players at the flight boundary getting added to two flights
  • Fix purses not showing per flight in team-format tournaments
  • Fix CTP fees when multiple par-3s share a single purse
  • Fix GHIN handicap refresh when a league member is missing a WHS account
  • Fix natural birdie not winning the tie-break against a net eagle
  • Fix course search results getting pushed off-screen
  • Fix stale registration fees on tournament cards when purses are added
  • Various other small fixes and polish
6.5.1 May 13, 2026
  • Ask Squabbit AI: a new in-app help chatbot that can answer questions about how the app works
  • Print hole competition sign-up sheets along with the rest of your tournament print assets
  • Pick multiple holes at once when adding hole competitions, with a satellite-view hole picker
  • Bulk-copy selected users' email addresses to your clipboard from the admin user list
  • Allow players to pick any tee on the course when player course selection is enabled
  • Honor the player's tee pick when adding a new course mid-round
  • Fix Quota countback using 18-hole stroke fractions in 9-hole rounds
  • Pair Six-Six-Six matchplay segments by handicap instead of arbitrary set order
  • Use rounded Course Handicap for singles matchplay strokes-off
  • Auto-add a linked ghost to the player they're subbing for in team formats
  • Credit ghost-substitute points to the league team in individual tournaments
  • Restrict FedEx event points rank to players in the same flight
  • Scope cart sign QR codes to just the players on that cart
  • Allow duplicate course names if the existing course is more than 20km away
  • Stop scroll-jump when editing long home text widgets
  • Fix unresponsive buttons on the Get help on Reddit dialog (web)
6.4.6 – 6.5.0 May 6, 2026
  • Track every shot from your wrist with the Apple Watch and Wear OS apps: pick lie + club, see live distance from your shot's start, end with stop, penalty, or finish hole
  • Shot tracking on the phone too: save shots from the map, see club markers + flight paths on each hole, and auto-derive FIR/GIR from your tracked shots
  • New Bag screen with custom clubs, manufacturer/model/loft, and a brand logo catalog
  • Match history vs another player on the Rounds tab: head-to-head record with anyone you've played
  • Ghost players: stand in for an absent matchplay opponent, with their points still rolling up to the player they're subbing for
  • Connections page consolidating Followers and Following into a tabbed view
  • Profile redesign with a scorecard-style header, topical stat chips, and a shareable QR code (plus a scanner to find other players)
  • Tied leaderboard placements now open a dialog explaining the tiebreaker
  • Best Front 9 and Best Back 9 tournament formats now honor your tiebreak method
  • Smart shuffle considers prior league event rounds and no longer drops odd-one-out players in odd-sized tee times
  • Per-tournament-round quota overrides
  • "One team per scorecard" option for team-format tournaments
  • Schedule rows now show the weather, with a per-user temperature-unit setting (defaults from your locale)
  • Soft pastel scoring badges on the scorecard
  • Account merge flow now supports both authenticated and search-picker (non-auth) accounts
  • Substitute players in team leagues now attribute their scores to the subbed-for player on the leaderboard
  • Player-last-names team name option
  • Thai baht currency support
  • Many polish + bug fixes: 9-hole playing handicap when SI #1 is on the unplayed nine, 666 tournament purse splitting, league start/end date displays, and more
